Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 16 oz ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef, onion, and garlic until browned. Drain excess fat.
  3. Add the marinara sauce, salt, and pepper, then simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  5. In a baking dish, layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta, and mozzarella. Repeat layers as desired.
  6. Top with Parmesan cheese and fresh basil if using.
  7. Bake for 25-30 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  8. Let cool for 10 minutes before serving.
